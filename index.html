Analyzing Drinking Behavior in Teens

For this project, we wanted to assess risk behavior in teens. We used a dataset looking at alcohol consumption in Portuguese teenagers. We wanted to break down our project into two sections: Who is more likely to engage in elevated drinking habits? And what are the consequences of this high risk behavior?

The first aspect we wanted to look at for who is more likely to drink is how family dynamics are related to weekend drinking habits. We looked at the distribution of weekend alcohol consumption in relation to whether or not parents are still together and whether or not the student lives in an urban or rural environment.
